Summary：

The P9800S Cement Analyzer is X-ray Fluorescence for everyone in the cement industry to analyze nine elements (Cl, K, Si, Mg, Al, S, P, Ca, Fe) in cement raw clinker at one time and automatically calculate the elemental composition of raw materials. This product is the only one in China that adopts the upper-illuminated optical path structure, which overcomes the problem of powder-like ash pollution detector and light pipe, improves measurement accuracy and is easy to do maintain. It has a sample liftable test platform and a sample spin system, the lifting platform automatically puts down the sample at the end of the measurement to facilitate the replacement of the sample, and the X-ray does not illuminate the person, which is safe and reliable. With a monitored sample spin system, the measurement deviation caused by sample non-uniformity can be eliminated and the stability of the measurement can be improved.

 

Applications：

Cement manufacturing industry: raw materials, raw materials, clinker, cement and other ingredients analysis;

Limestone industry: Limestone various composition analysis.
