Steel industry

As the most fundamental engineering and construction material steel plays and important role in the modern world. Steel is the most commonly used metal in multiple sectors of infrastructure, automotives, railways, offshore and marine plates, skyscrapers and machineries. This metal comes in different grades and 4 main types of carbon steels, alloy steels, stainless steels, tool steels which are 100% recyclable.

As a result, the elemental analysis of this metal is really critical in all the production processes. Combined LIBS instrumentations and machine learning method is exploited for steel scrap classifications during recycling processes. Real-time measurements alongside a low or no sample preparation makes the technique adaptable to be exploited in industrial fields. Applications of LIBS analyzers in steel industry sector extends from preprocessing phase of iron, production lines and QC/QA departments.

Spectoshimatsu systems could be used to characterize different types of iron ores of hematite, magnetite, goethite, limonite, etc. before entering the process lines. Quantitative in-situ analysis of decisive elements (C, Ni, Mn, Cr,…) in steel grading are fulfilled with high sensitivity using LIBS systems.Steel alloy properties such as yield strength, corrosion resistance, hardness and toughness depend mainly on concentration weight of trace elements. Spectoshimatsu analyzers are able to detect the trace elements (P, S, C, V, Mn,…) in cast iron, low alloy, stainless and other types of steel up to ppm/ppb levels.

With the ability of positive material identification (PMI), Spectoshimatsu LIBS analyzers are capable of steel coating assessments. As the side application LIBS provides the users to have a simultaneous cleaning of steel surface corrosions and defects’ detections.
