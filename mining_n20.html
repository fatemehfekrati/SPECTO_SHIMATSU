Cement

Specto Shimatsu supplies multi-element analysis of raw meal, clinker, and cement.

Cement production enterprises have extremely strict product quality control. Raw meal batching and clinker calcination are the two core links in the entire production process. In the new dry process cement production technology, to ensure the stability of the calcination process and improve kiln output and clinker quality, requirements for raw meal uniformity and control of elemental contents are very strict.

To meet these process conditions, timely and accurate analysis and control of the chemical composition of raw fuels is necessary. Traditional chemical analysis methods cannot keep up with production speed; X-ray fluorescence (XRF) analyzers and fast spectrometric methods enable true process control instead of post-event monitoring, preventing costly deviations.
