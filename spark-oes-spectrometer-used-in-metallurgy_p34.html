Online LIBS Analyzer :


The technology provides simultaneous quantitative analysis of bulk material flows directly on the conveyor belt or in pipes. All elements,

including the light ones (C, Si, Mg, Al, etc..) can be detected in real-time mode without lab sampling and sample preparation.

Cross belt analyzers are designed to withstand the harsh industrial environment and are used in mining, beneficiation, raw material preparation and product quality control applications by clients in N. America, Europe, and Asia.



Industries:

Due to the nature of laser spectroscopy (LIBS) online analyzers successfully used in real-time quantitative analysis of various

elements of interest,including the light ones. Besides, parameters such as material moisture, ash, calorific value can be detected.


 

We operate in the following industries:

• Fertilizers (phosphate, potassium, composite NPK – P, K, Na, moisture and others)

• Iron and Steal (iron ore and concentrate, sinter mix, limestone, coke -Fe, Si, Ca, Mg, Mn, C, moisture and others)

• Refractories ( Mg, Si, Ca, Fe, Al, Cr, B, Mn and others)

• Coal (C, ash content, volatiles, moisture – Fe, Al, Si, Mg, Ca…)

• Industrial Minerals (quartz, clays, nepheline…)

• Base metals (Cu, Al, Co, Mo, Zn and others)

• Bauxite and Alumina

• Cement (limestone, raw meal – Ca, Si, Al, Fe…)
