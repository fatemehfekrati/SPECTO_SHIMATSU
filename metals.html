Metals

Specto Shimatsu offers a full range of solutions for the entire metals industry by its metal analyzer systems.

The scope of these solutions is very wide including the analysis of trace elements, heavy and toxic metals, molten metals, etc.

Specto Shimatsu metal analyzers are exploited in different production, safety or quality assurance (QA) processes to detect, sort, and characterize the metallic targets.

These LIBS based systems are suitable to be used in harsh and hazardous environments such as metallography furnaces or chemical plants.

The inaccessible sites could be also characterized by Specto Shimatsu instrumentations with high limit of detection (LOD).

This remote analysis is used for real-time classification of the scraps such as aluminum, steel and other common metals.

Ref: Noll, Reinhard, et al. "LIBS analyses for industrial applications–an overview of developments from 2014 to 2018." Journal of Analytical Atomic Spectrometry 33.6 (2018): 945-956.

Regarding the high accuracy of the technique, the stationary models of LIBS analyzers are used for quality control during metal processing over conveyer belt as well as in laboratories for research and development.

As the dominant privilege this method is applicable for online/inline detection of trace metals (order of ppm or ppb) in different base materials of solid, liquid and air.

This remote analysis is used for real-time classification of the scraps such as aluminum, steel and other common metals.

Also, metal analyzer of Specto Shimatsu is able for elemental analysis of heavy metals without any need for sample preparation.

This application is highly important in quality control of water and soil as well as agricultural products. It is noteworthy that this method is rapid for simultaneous elemental characterization of multi metals such as steel, copper, zinc, bronze, lead, aluminum, beryllium, etc. regardless of their state or quantity.
