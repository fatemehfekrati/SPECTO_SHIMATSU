Brief description

JYBO Handheld XRF spectrometers use X-ray fluorescence technology to analysis the composition of a sample material by measuring the characteristic X-ray energies of the elements in the sample. Different characteristic X-rays of elements are excited when X-rays irradiate the sample. The type and amounts of elements in the sample can be determined by detecting the energy and amount of these characteristic X-rays.

Analytical speed and accuracy: with fast analytical speed and accuracy. In a short time, it can quickly determine the content of many elements in the sample with high precision and accuracy.

Wide application range: widely used in geology, minerals, materials, environmental protection and other fields. It can be used to analysis the types and contents of elements in various metal materials, alloys, soils, ores, ceramics and other samples. Meanwhile, it can also be used to analysis harmful elements in packaging materials, elements restricted by RoHS directive and so on.

Advantages: Handheld XRF spectrometer has the advantages of portability, speed, accuracy and versatility. It can be analyzed anytime, anywhere, without the need for complicated sample preparation process, and can get the analysis results quickly. At the same time, the instrument also has low maintenance costs and long service life, while fast, accurate, portable, multi-functional and other advantages, widely used in various fields.

 

Unmatched and unique performance

1. Using a completely redesigned ray tube, no high voltage power lines, no RF noise, and better X-ray shielding.

2. More precise construction, shortening the distance between the ray tube, detector and the sample under test, increasing the signal by 40% for some applications.

3. Heat dissipation: part of the body is designed with aluminium alloy shell, the instrument has a special slot heat sink, the whole system makes the heat dissipation very effective and prolongs the life of the machine, the X-ray analyzer is more stable and thus the failure rate is extremely low.

4. Standardization: the instrument does not need to be standardized for start-up, it can be tested directly, standardization is only optional.

5. Instrument case using aluminium alloy, plastic design, durable, soft rubber handle design, feel good, more suitable for long time use.

6. Gravity inversion: the instrument has a great balance, in the test can stand on the workbench, do not need to support the hand, a key button design, even if a long time to operate without fatigue.

7. Detection window: the window explosion-proof film design, you can directly test any shape of items such as shavings, steel wire and other irregular items. (Detection window is optional)

Technical performance

1. Truly non-destructive, fast and accurate detection in the field, directly displaying the percentage content of the elements.

2. The alloy analyzer is simply placed in direct contact with the surface of the alloy to be tested, and the alloy grade can be determined on site without waiting or spending time.

3. The sample to be tested can be alloy block, alloy sheet, alloy wire, alloy slag, alloy powder.

4. Compensatory test method for irregular or small samples can detect very small samples, such as 0.05mm diameter wire can be immediately recognized.

5. Pistol-type extendable probe design can be used to detect the inside of pipelines, weld seams, and other locations.

6. High-temperature materials can be detected up to 250°C.

7. Alloy grades can be added, edited and deleted from the library on site.

8. Fastest analysis speed in the world, identifying alloying elements in just 1 second.

9. Customized Android 11 operating system for program flexibility.

10. No need to use a computer, you can specify, view and zoom in on the spectra of the relevant elements on the spot.

11. Protection level: IP54; all-in-one design, soft rubber and plastic parts of the convex groove & groove construction design, so that the instrument has a very good three anti-performance, can withstand harsh working environment, fog, rain, dusty workplace can also work normally.

12. Repeatability testing: with the average value of multiple tests statistical function, can effectively improve the testing accuracy of the instrument.

13. Electromagnetic interference is shielded, even near mobile phones or two-way wireless communication devices can work normally.

Display

Data display
