Environmental

 

Environmental applications of Sepectoshimatsu LIBS analyzers include direct inspection of contaminations/pollutions in environment such as soil, rocks, sands, sediments, rivers or air.

 

On the other hand LIBS scanners could be employed in monitoring of industrial pollutants: electronic wastes, landfill leachates, lubricating oils and aerosols. Computational methods as efficient chemometric, calibration and machine learning tools are applied in manipulation of environmental LIBS data for material identification, discrimination and classification.

 

Soil: Elemental analysis of soil is based on determination of different metals’ content of Al, Ca, Fe, and Cu. Besides, soil classification is attainable with quantification of soil physical and chemical properties, including pH, texture, carbon, nutrient and toxic elements, spatial elemental mapping and humification degree. Detection of soil/rock pollution by trace elements of Ti, Cr, As, Ag, Mo and Pb with LODs ˂ 10 ppm could be achieved by LIBS analyzers.

 

Rocks and sediments: In-situ discrimination and classification of rocks by geochemical imaging of deposits to prepare the mineral fingerprints. Sedimentary, metamorphic and igneous rocks are analyzed by LIBS analyzers for contaminations of Ba, Zn, Cr, Cu, and Pb traces.

 

Waters and Leachates: Spectoshimatsu LIBS analysers have been employed for water quality monitoring, including the analysis of trace elements and contaminants in water samples and landfill leachates. Dominant factors to be quantified in aqueous solutions are the P, N, C heavy metals, chemical oxygen demand (COD), NOX and SOX.

 

Air and exhaust gas: LIBS has been used for the analysis of elemental composition in aerosols, contributing to air quality monitoring and pollution source discrimination. Spectoshimatsu analysers could be employed for analyzing the exhaust gas to measure air-to-fuel ratio or CO2 content. Multiple aerosols as suspended heavy metals as cobalt or nickel or other contaminations as the subsidiaries of fossil fuel combustion could be targeted.

 

Waste analysis & recycling: Spectoshimatsu analyzers are applicable in sorting and categorizing different types of waste materials covering plastic, glass or even organic wastes by means of recycling and optimized deposit managements. Accordingly, different plastic types LDPE, HDPE PS, PVC, PET, PC could be classified. Moreover, LIBS has been utilized for the analysis of electronic waste, aiding in the identification and quantification of metals in electronic components.
