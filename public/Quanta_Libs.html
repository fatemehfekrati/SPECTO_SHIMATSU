The Quanta LIBS system is a compact laboratory with minimal maintenance requirements that is functional in diverse industrial sites, harsh environments and landscapes.

    This portable compact LIBS analyzer as a user-friendly equipment which requires minimum or no sample preparation could be applied in inventory surveys, alloy characterizations as well as QC/QA inspections. Besides, positive material identification (PMI) and scrap recycling are demanding sectors for Quanta LIBS gun.

    The spectral window around 1.6um of built-in laser wavelength is about 8000 times more eye-safe than other laser irradiations. Not only the method avoids the hazardous X radiations but also capacitate to track the traces of C, Li and other light elements versus expensive XRF testing. Also, Quanta LIBS provides the users with adaptable detection limits as a requirement for grade identification. The verified LOD for many elements such as Fe, Zn, Cu, Mn, Cr, Sn, Ti, Zr, Ni, etc. stands ˂ 10 ppm.
