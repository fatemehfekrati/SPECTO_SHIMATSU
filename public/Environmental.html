Environmental

Environmental applications of Sepectoshimatsu LIBS analyzers include direct inspection of contaminations/pollutions in environment such as soil, rocks, sands, sediments, rivers or air. On the other hand, LIBS scanners could be employed in monitoring of industrial pollutants: electronic wastes, landfill leachates, lubricating oils and aerosols. Computational methods as efficient chemometric, calibration and machine learning tools are applied in manipulation of environmental LIBS data for material identification, discrimination and classification.

Soil: Elemental analysis of soil is based on tracking different metals’ content such as Al, Ca, Fe, and Cu. Besides, soil classification is attainable with quantification of soil physical and chemical properties, including humification degree, pH, texture, carbon, nutrient and toxic elements. Detection of soil/rock pollution by trace elements (Ti, Cr, As, Ag, Mo, Pb, etc.) with LODs ˂ 10 ppm could be achieved through spatial elemental mapping by LIBS analyzers.

Rocks and sediments: In-situ discrimination and classification of rocks is performed by geochemical imaging of deposits in order to prepare the mineral fingerprints. Sedimentary, metamorphic and igneous rocks are inspected by LIBS analyzers for contaminations of Ba, Zn, Cr, Cu, and Pb metals.

Waters and leachates: Spectoshimatsu LIBS analysers have been employed for water quality monitoring to detect trace elements and contaminants in water samples and landfill leachates. Primary traces to be quantified in aqueous solutions are P, N, C heavy metals, chemical oxygen demand (COD), NOX and SOX.

Air and exhaust gas: LIBS has been used to analyze pollution indices and aerosols; contributing to the assessment of air quality index (AQI). Spectoshimatsu systems could be employed to control the exhaust gas by measuring air-to-fuel ratio or CO2 content. Furthermore, multiple aerosols of suspended heavy metals such as cobalt, nickel or other contaminations as the subsidiaries of fossil fuel combustion could be targeted.

Waste analysis & recycling: Spectoshimatsu analyzers are applicable in sorting and categorizing different types of waste materials covering plastic, glass or even organic wastes by means of recycling and optimized deposit managements. Accordingly, different plastic types LDPE, HDPE PS, PVC, PET, PC could be classified. Moreover, LIBS has been utilized for the analysis of electronic wastes to facilitate the recycling procedure.
