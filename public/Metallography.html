Metallography

Specto Shimatsu offers a full range of solutions for the metal industry by its benchtop, scanner or portable elemental analyzers. The scope of these solutions is very wide including the analysis of trace elements, heavy and toxic metals, molten metals, etc.

Specto Shimatsu systems are exploited in different production, safety or quality assurance (QA) processes to detect, sort, and characterize the metallic targets. These LIBS based systems are suitable to be used in harsh and hazardous environments such as metallography furnaces or chemical plants. The inaccessible sites could be also characterized by Specto Shimatsu instrumentations with high limit of detection (LOD). This remote analysis is used for real-time classification of the scraps such as aluminum, steel and other common metals.

Regarding the high accuracy of the technique, the stationary models of LIBS analyzers are used for quality control during metal processing over conveyer belt as well as in laboratories for research and development. Dominant privilege of this method is online/inline detection of trace metals (order of ppm or ppb) in different phases of solid, liquid and gaseous. Also, LIBS analyzer of Specto Shimatsu is capable of elemental analysis of heavy metals without or negligible sample preparation. This capability is of great importance regarding the detection of environmental pollutants or even authentication of different gems.

It is noteworthy that this method is rapid for simultaneous elemental characterization of multi metals cush as steel, copper, zinc, bronze, lead, aluminum, beryllium, etc. regardless of their state or quantity. Online LIBS scanners monitor the core metals’ properties with high speed and resolution during the production process.
